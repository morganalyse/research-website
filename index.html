Motorola DynaTAC: 1983

DynaTAC is a series of cellular device manufactured by Motorola, from 1983. It took roughly 30 hours to charge. This mobile phone could also hold up to 30 minutes of available talking time. There are many different things to describe this cellphone. It weighed about 784g and it was not touchscreen, the dimensions were 3300 x 898 x 445mm, and its volume was .1318710 cm3
